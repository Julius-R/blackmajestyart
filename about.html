About Me

My name is Markeisha Shorter. I’m a twenty-three year old artist, born and raised in Louisiana. I started drawing and painting as a toddler, and it quickly became the biggest part of my life. Since then, I’ve dedicated my time to perfecting my craft, hoping to share my creations with art lovers around the globe. One day, I hope to be a graphic designer as well. I believe that the world is filled with beauty, and I try to reflect that through my art. For me, this isn’t just a hobby. It’s what I love.
